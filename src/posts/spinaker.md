This is a story of how I participated in an international student program dedicated to energy topics.
We discussed existing energy issues, like decentralized energy supply, dependence on electricity, and alternative energy. But all of that is tedious, so let's skip to the fun part.

# AGH University of Science and Technology
The science part of our program was conducted at AGH University of Science and Technology. This is a technical university in Krakow, Poland, founded in 1913, and is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the country.

A large, classical hall that exists to impress you before the rest of the buildings get a chance to.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/agh_hall.jpg)
Next stop was the geological museum.
The highlight was a collection of fluorescent minerals. The stand had two lamps to show under regular light and ultraviolet.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/agh_geomuseum_uv.jpg)

The rest of the collection was equally impressive, with geodes, malachite, agate, and more.

# City of Krakow
Separate mention goes to the city where AGH is located. Krakow is a beautiful city in southern Poland.

In its center stands the Sukiennice, a medieval cloth hall rebuilt in Renaissance style, which served as the main trading point for merchants for centuries and still stands in the middle of the Main Market Square today.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/krakow_sukiennice.jpg)

Wawel Royal Castle sits on a hill above the city, with the Vistula River running below.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/krakow_1.jpg)
![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/krakow_river.jpg)

The castle complex also includes Wawel Cathedral, where Polish kings were crowned. The Sigismund Tower houses one of the largest bells in Poland.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/krakow_2.jpg)
![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/krakow_3.jpg)

Nearby, the Collegium Novum serves as the main administrative building of the Jagiellonian University, one of the oldest universities in the world.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/krakow_jagiellonian_university.jpg)

This was also the first time that I tried ramen.

![](/posts/materials/posts/spinaker/ramen.jpg)
